    Dr. Ayesha Jabeen is one of MediTrust’s leading heart specialists, dedicated to diagnosing and treating cardiovascular diseases with compassion and precision. Her focus is on preventive cardiology, lifestyle management, and early detection of heart conditions. Her expertise is highly valuable for MediTrust patients as she helps improve cardiac health through advanced diagnostic tools and personalized treatment plans — ensuring every heartbeat counts.

    Dr. Ahmed Farooq

    Orthopedic Surgeon

    Dr. Ahmed Farooq brings years of surgical expertise in bone, joint, and muscle treatments.
